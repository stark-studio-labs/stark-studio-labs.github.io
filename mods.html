Economy Sim

In Development

A real economy for your Sims world. Prices change based on supply and demand. Businesses compete. Neighborhoods get richer or poorer depending on what happens in them.

What it adds

  • Dynamic pricing that responds to how Sims spend money
  • Businesses that compete, expand, and sometimes fail
  • Real estate values that shift based on neighborhood activity
  • Job market with salaries tied to the local economy
  • Stock market where your Sims can invest (and lose) Simoleons
