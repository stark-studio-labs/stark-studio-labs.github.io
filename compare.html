Honest Comparisons

How We Stack Up.

We respect the modders who came before us. Here's an honest look at what exists, what we're building, and where we think we can do better.

These mods are built on the shoulders of giants. MCCC, Slice of Life, WickedWhims, and dozens of others created the Sims modding community. We're not here to replace them — we're here to build the infrastructure they deserve. Some of our mods complement existing ones. Some offer alternatives. We'll always be honest about which is which.

Being Honest

What we don't do (yet).

Adult content

We're focused on simulation depth, not adult interactions. WickedWhims and Basemental serve that audience well. We're not competing there.

Tray management

Sims 4 Tray Importer is excellent at what it does. We handle mods, not tray files.

Years of battle testing

MCCC has been stable through dozens of game patches. We're new. Our testing is thorough, but we haven't survived a major EA update yet.

Massive content packs

Mods like Slice of Life add hundreds of interactions, buffs, and events. Our approach is different — fewer handcrafted events, more emergent behavior.
