How it Works

Three steps. Zero headaches.

Download

Grab the installer for your OS. Open it and drag to Applications (Mac) or run the installer (Windows).

Install

Stark Labs auto-detects your Sims 4 Mods folder. Browse, search, and install mods with one click.

Play

Launch The Sims 4. Stark Labs handles conflicts, load order, and updates in the background. Just play.

Requirements

System requirements.

macOS

  • macOS 10.15 Catalina or later
  • The Sims 4 installed (EA App or Steam)
  • 4 GB RAM minimum
  • 200 MB disk space

Windows

  • Windows 10 (64-bit) or later
  • The Sims 4 installed (EA App or Steam)
  • 4 GB RAM minimum
  • 200 MB disk space
